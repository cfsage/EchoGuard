AI Canary for Information Authenticity

Welcome to EchoGuard!

Thank you for installing EchoGuard. This extension helps you identify potentially misleading content by automatically analyzing web pages as you browse.

Automatic Analysis

Every webpage you visit is automatically analyzed for credibility and emotional language patterns to help you make informed decisions about the content.

Visual Warnings

Warning banners appear at the top of pages from unreliable sources or with highly emotional content, helping you stay aware of potential misinformation.

Detailed Insights

Click the EchoGuard icon in your browser toolbar to view detailed analysis, including the specific emotional keywords detected and information about the source.

How to Use EchoGuard

  1. Browse Normally - EchoGuard works automatically in the background, analyzing each page you visit.
  2. Watch for Warning Banners - Warning banners will appear at the top of pages that have credibility issues or use emotional language.
  3. Check the Details - Click the EchoGuard icon in your browser toolbar for a detailed breakdown of the analysis.
  4. Toggle AUTO Mode - Enable or disable automatic analysis using the toggle in the popup if you prefer manual control.

EchoGuard is designed to be unobtrusive but informative, helping you navigate the web with greater awareness of potentially misleading content.
